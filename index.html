RandomKit
Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

RandomKit ("the App") is a free utility app for iOS that provides randomization tools including dice rolls, coin flips, number generators, and more. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Information We Collect

RandomKit itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All randomization happens entirely on your device.

Advertising

The App displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following data to serve and personalize ads:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA), subject to your ATT consent
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address / approximate location
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

When the App first launches, you will be shown Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If you decline, only non-personalized ads will be shown and your IDFA will not be used.

For details on how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data from apps that use AdMob.

Third-Party SDKs

The App uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK. No other third-party analytics or tracking SDKs are included.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
